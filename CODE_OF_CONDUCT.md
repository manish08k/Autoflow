# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in AutoFlow a respectful, inclusive, and collaborative experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

* Be respectful and professional.
* Welcome constructive feedback.
* Focus on improving the project.
* Help new contributors learn and grow.
* Communicate clearly and respectfully.

## Unacceptable Behavior

* Harassment or discrimination.
* Personal attacks or insults.
* Trolling or disruptive behavior.
* Publishing private information.
* Any conduct that negatively impacts the community.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing community standards.

Repeated violations may result in warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent bans from the project.

## Contact

If you experience unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainers.
